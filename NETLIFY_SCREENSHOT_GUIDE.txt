================================================
NETLIFY DASHBOARD NAVIGATION GUIDE
Step-by-step with what you'll see
================================================

STEP 1: Login and Find Your Site
================================
URL: https://app.netlify.com

What you'll see:
- Your list of sites/projects
- Find the one with your project name or the auto-generated name
- Click on it


STEP 2: Navigate to Site Settings
==================================
At the top of your site dashboard, you'll see tabs:
[ Site overview ] [ Deploys ] [ Site settings ] [ Domain settings ] ...

Click: [ Site settings ]


STEP 3: Find Environment Variables
===================================
On the left sidebar, you'll see:
- General
- Build & deploy
- Domain management
- Environment variables  ← CLICK THIS
- Identity
- Forms
- Analytics
...


STEP 4: Add Variables Page
===========================
You should now see a section titled "Environment variables"

If you have no variables yet:
   "No environment variables yet"
   [Add a variable] button

If you have variables:
   List of existing variables
   [Add a variable] button at top right

Click: [Add a variable]

STEP 8: Trigger New Deploy
===========================
Click the "Deploys" tab at the top

You'll see:
[ Site overview ] [ Deploys ] [ Site settings ] ...
                      ^
                   CLICK HERE

Then click the "Trigger deploy" button (top right)
Select: "Deploy site"

You'll see:
- "Deploy in progress..."
- Build log scrolling
- After 2-3 minutes: "Published" with green checkmark


STEP 9: Visit Your Site
========================
Once deploy shows "Published":
- Click on your site URL at the top
- Or click "Open production deploy"

Your site should now load correctly!

================================================

TROUBLESHOOTING:
================

Q: I don't see "Environment variables" in the left sidebar
A: Make sure you're in "Site settings" tab, not "Site overview"

Q: The values have quotes around them
A: Remove the quotes - paste the values directly without quotes

Q: Deploy succeeded but site still blank
A: Clear browser cache or try incognito mode

Q: Still not working
A: Open browser console (F12) and share the error message
